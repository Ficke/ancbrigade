How are ANC commissioners elected?

You elect your ANC Commissioner every two years in non-partisan elections. To run for a commissioner seat, you need 25 signatures from neighbors in your Single Member District to put your name on the ballot. Candidates for ANC commissioner are allowed to raise contributions of up to $25 from an individual or entity.

ANC campaigns are typically small, grassroots efforts; winning candidates often run unopposed and receive just a few hundred votes. In fact, many commissioner seats are currently vacant and many ANCs are looking for volunteers to help with various projects.

Visit the DC Board of Elections website to learn more about running for ANC commissioner.
